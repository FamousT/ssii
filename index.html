About Us

SSII - raising shining stars

Shining Stars Initiative International (SSII) is a mentoring and capacity building forum for teenagers and youths.
Read More

Our Philisophy

Purposeful mentorship and right relationships, combined with deliberate followership and willingness to learn which is believed would put a youth on the right path to success.

Our Vision

To raise purpose and passion driven young men and women to become world phenomena and shining stars in character, conduct and competence.

Our Mission

To impart the teenagers and youth with the positive values and right attitude to life, that will enable them become responsible and accountable adults who can rebuild the broken walls of this nation across their fields of endeavour and spheres of influence; changing the world positively in their academics, career and business.
